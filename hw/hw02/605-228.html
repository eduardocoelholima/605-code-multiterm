Homework Description:

Sometimes it would be helpful if a wordle player could ask if particular characters are part of the word. You have to add this functionality.

Your output does not have to be identical to mine, but similar.

Your Work:

Your implementation has to accept an input of the folling form ’+....’, a plus sign followed by exactly 4 characters. Your program has to indicate if the 4 character are part of the word to guess or not.

An execution of a possible solution looks like:

java Wordle
_ indicates the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
* indicates the letter is in the word and correct spot.
x indicates the letter is not in the word.
Try to guess the word in 5 tries.
    xxxxx
1 > +oieu
        o is part of the word
        e is part of the word
    xxxxx
2 > boxer
boxer was guessed correctly after 2 guesses.

Idea for a Solution:
